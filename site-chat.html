Custom Sections in Google Chat: Organize Your Conversations Easily

Published on February 05, 2025

Google Chat has just received an update that promises to transform the way you organize your conversations. Now, with Custom Sections, you can categorize your conversations into different personalized sections, improving visibility and productivity.

This new feature allows users to group messages and spaces into customized categories. This way, it is possible to find important conversations more quickly and keep the Google Chat interface more organized.
